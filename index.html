Parana

Paraná is a state in southern Brazil known for its diverse geography, strong economy, and rich cultural heritage. Its capital, Curitiba, is a major urban center celebrated for urban planning and sustainability.Economically, Paraná ranks among Brazil’s top states, driven by agriculture (soy, corn, coffee), industry, and energy production, notably the Itaipu Dam on the Paraná River.
